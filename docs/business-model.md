# Business Model: Independent Road & Bridge Inspection Robotics

## Classification

- Repository: `cloud-itonami-cofog-04.5`
- COFOG: `04.5` (Transport)
- Activity: pavement and bridge-structure condition survey and reporting
- Social impact: earlier defect detection, safer roads/bridges,
  transparent public-works spend justification

## Customer

- municipal public-works / transportation departments
- toll-road and bridge authorities
- engineering consultancies performing periodic asset audits
- rural counties without an in-house inspection fleet

## Offer

- scheduled + on-demand road-segment / bridge-structure survey
- defect-severity report with imaging/sensor evidence
- prioritized repair recommendation queue
- immediate-closure-worthy defect escalation (human sign-off, never
  automated)
- condition-history audit export for capital-planning justification

## Revenue

- per-segment / per-structure survey fee
- municipal or authority subscription (recurring inspection contract)
- capital-planning report package for grant/budget applications
- compliance/audit export package

## Trust Controls

- repair-priority recommendations require Infrastructure Inspection
  Governor clearance
- any `:high`/`:safety-critical` structural finding always escalates to
  human sign-off (closure decisions are never automated)
- every survey, finding and escalation is logged
- public condition claims must reference measured sensor data
